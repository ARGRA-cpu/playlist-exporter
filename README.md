Playlist Exporter 🎵
A free, privacy-first tool for saving your Spotify playlists as CSV, HTML, or plain text files.
Try it live →
---
What it does
Copy any Spotify playlist, paste it in, and export it instantly — no login, no account, no data stored anywhere. Everything runs locally in your browser.
Export formats:
CSV — opens directly in Excel, Google Sheets, or Numbers
HTML — a clean, styled page you can save and keep forever
Plain text — a simple numbered list that works anywhere
Clipboard — paste directly into any app or document
Works with playlists of any size, including thousands of songs.
---
Why I built this
I have playlists with thousands of songs and wanted a simple way to export and reference them — without handing over my Spotify login to a third-party app. I also wanted a hands-on project to learn web development.
This was built entirely with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no dependencies, no server.
---
How to use it
Open Spotify on desktop and navigate to any playlist
Click a song, then press `Ctrl+A` (Windows) or `Cmd+A` (Mac) to select all tracks
Copy with `Ctrl+C` / `Cmd+C`
Paste into the tool and hit Parse playlist
Choose your export format

How it works
1

Open your Spotify playlist

Works on desktop Spotify (Windows or Mac). Open any playlist — yours or one you follow.

2

Select and copy all tracks

Click any song in the list, then press Ctrl+A (Windows) or Cmd+A (Mac) to select everything. Then Ctrl+C / Cmd+C to copy.

3

Paste it above

Click the text area above and press Ctrl+V / Cmd+V to paste. Hit "Parse playlist" and your tracks will appear instantly.

4

Export in your preferred format

Download as a CSV for Excel, a styled HTML page to keep, plain text, or just copy to your clipboard. All free, all instant.
